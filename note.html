Gardening Tips for Beginners

Tue Mar 19 2024

I've always wanted to start a garden, but I'm not sure where to begin. I'll start by researching the best plants for my climate and soil type. I'll also learn about the basics of gardening, such as how to prepare the soil, when to plant, and how to care for plants. I'll keep a journal of my garden's progress, noting when each plant blooms and any challenges I encounter. I'll also plan to attend a local gardening workshop to learn more tips and tricks from experienced gardeners.
